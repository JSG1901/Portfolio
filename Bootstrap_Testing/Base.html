Health and happiness
Time with friends, or to laugh, or to simply forget (or ignore!) the stresses of your day are critical to ongoing self-health. Try to get outside. Even a few minutes in the sun builds vitamin D3 levels, critical to mental well-being, and laughing releases Dopamine, the pleasure drug in our brains. Nothing sounds funny? Even fake laughing releases Dopamine, so fake it until you make it!

Take a little time for yourself. This is an h1-sized headline that wraps.
